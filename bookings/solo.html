For Production & Venue Staff

Technical Rider

Audio and stage requirements for both performance configurations. Please share with the front-of-house engineer and venue technical team in advance of the event.

Violin & Keyboard / Ableton

Hybrid Performance Configuration
Audio Outputs
2× 1/4" TRS line outputs (stereo L/R) from Apollo Twin. Apollo Twin provided by Julie Schatz.
Staging
1× small table or equipment riser (approx. 24" × 24" or larger) at the performer's station, holding laptop, audio interface, wireless receiver, and effects pedal.
Power
1× power strip with a minimum of 4 outlets at the performer's station (laptop, Apollo, BLX4 receiver, Zoom pedal).
Monitoring
1× floor wedge monitor. Where possible, please provide a "Main Mix" send so the performer hears the blended balance of violin and Ableton tracks.

Violin Only

Acoustic Performance Configuration
Audio Output
1× 1/4" mono instrument output from the Zoom effects pedal.
DI Requirements
Venue to provide 1× mono DI box and an XLR cable to the front-of-house console.
Staging
1× small table or riser (approx. 24" × 24") at the performer's station, holding wireless receiver and effects pedal.
Power
1× power strip with a minimum of 2 outlets (BLX4 wireless receiver and Zoom pedal).
Monitoring
1× floor wedge monitor.

Notes for the Engineer

Processing
For the violin, please apply standard EQ and compression at the console to smooth out the electric violin transients. A high-pass filter at ~180 Hz is appreciated.
Effects
Performer is using a Zoom pedal for core tone. Please add a tasteful Hall Reverb on the house mix to suit the room acoustics.
Monitoring
Please provide a clear monitor mix. If the house reverb makes intonation difficult to hear, keep the monitor mix dry (no house reverb) while keeping the house mix wet.
