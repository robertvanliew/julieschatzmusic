For Production & Venue Staff

Technical Rider

A blueprint for high-energy hybrid sets: DJ DX on decks with live violin and keyboard. Please share with the production team and venue tech well ahead of the event.

Audio Foundation

Sound & Booth
House PA
2× 18" subwoofers for heavy bass coverage. House FOH sized to room capacity.
DJ Booth
One 6' vibration-free table dedicated to the DJ rig.
Power
Two dedicated 15-amp surge-protected circuits at the booth.

Live Hybrid Integration

Violin & Keyboard
DI Boxes
Active DI boxes provided by the venue for live violin and keyboard inputs to FOH.
Stage Power
Dedicated power feed at the live performer's station, separate from the DJ booth circuits.
Cabling
XLR runs from the DI boxes to front-of-house. Cable lengths confirmed at advance with the venue.

Stage & Communication

Logistics & Flow
Outdoor
Outdoor setups must be fully covered to protect gear from sun, rain, and heat.
Microphones
Two wireless handhelds and one straight stand provided for announcements and host moments.
Load-In
Coordinated with the venue's runner. Soundcheck window confirmed in advance.

Hospitality

Hydration
Room-temperature still water at the booth and the live performer's station throughout setup, soundcheck, and the set.
Catering
Healthy options with zero processed sugar: fresh fruit, raw nuts, vegetables, simple proteins. No energy drinks or candy on the rider.
