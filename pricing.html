Booking & Details

How It Works

Retainer & Contract

50% non-refundable retainer + signed performance agreement secures the date. Remaining balance due before the event.

Travel Zones

NYC bookings include travel (Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, Bronx, Staten Island, Hoboken, Jersey City). The inner ring covers Long Island, Westchester, Northern NJ, and Greenwich. Hudson Valley and beyond are custom-quoted.

Song Requests

Up to 3 songs from the existing repertoire are included with every booking. Custom arrangements outside the repertoire are an add-on; ideally booked 4–6 weeks ahead.

Sound System

Every booking includes a low-profile, high-fidelity PA scaled to the venue: wired XLR for indoor rooms, battery-powered amplification for outdoor lawns and rooftops, and wireless lavalier mics on request.

Response Time

Julie reviews every inquiry personally and responds within 24 hours. A short video call follows before any agreement is signed.

Lead Time

For Saturday weddings in peak season, 6–12 months out is typical. Off-season, weeknight, and corporate bookings often work on shorter timelines.
